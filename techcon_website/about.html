The History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a local technology meetup in San Francisco. Aimed at bringing together passionate developers, engineers, and thinkers, it quickly gained momentum. By 2015, TechCon had expanded into a national event, and today, it stands as one of the premier international tech conferences, attracting speakers and attendees from over 40 countries.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to foster innovation, learning, and collaboration across the technology sector. We strive to be an inclusive platform that champions breakthrough ideas, cultivates diversity, and inspires future generations of tech leaders.
