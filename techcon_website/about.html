The History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots meetup in San Francisco for developers and engineers to share ideas and collaborate. Over the years, it evolved into one of the world’s most anticipated technology conferences. Today, TechCon hosts thousands of attendees annually and features global leaders across tech disciplines.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to unite the global tech community through inspiration, education, and innovation. We believe in providing an inclusive platform that encourages dialogue, promotes diversity, and enables creators to build a better digital future.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Jane Smith – AI Researcher

Dr. Smith is a global leader in artificial intelligence and ethics. Her work focuses on building transparent and human-centered AI systems. She delivered the 2022 keynote on “Responsible AI in the Age of Automation.”

Elon Ray – Founder of FutureNet

Elon Ray is the visionary behind FutureNet, a project that aims to decentralize internet access. He has spoken at multiple TechCon events on open infrastructure and the social impact of digital inclusion.

Priya Desai – Cybersecurity Expert

Priya Desai is an expert in digital security and data rights. At TechCon 2023, she led a groundbreaking panel on privacy, AI regulations, and defending personal freedom in the digital era.
